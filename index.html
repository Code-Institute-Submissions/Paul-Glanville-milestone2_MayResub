Rules and instructions of the game.

  • To begin the game and start each round press the Start button.
  • Each box will light up in a sequence.
  • You must click the tiles after the sequence is finished to proceed to the next round.
  • If you click an incorrect tile, quit or reset; You will then have to restart from the beginning.
