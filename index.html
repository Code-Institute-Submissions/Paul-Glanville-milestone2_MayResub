Rules and instructions of the game.

  • to begin the game press the Start button.
  • each box will light up in a sequence.
  • You are then given the chance to repeat the sequence that you have been presented.
  • As you succeed with each stage the sequence will get slightly harder.
  • If you fail, quit or reset; You will then have to restart from the beginning.
